Philippine Consulate Juresdiction

Locating Your Nearest PH Consulate
Depending on the the location of your birth in the United States, each Philippine consulate has a specific
juresdiction over the area. Due to the pandemic, the processing times have been delayed. If you are someone
who resides in Washington State, contacting the consulate in Vancouver Canada or New York was most
helpful for me when filing my sisters report of birth
